**Week 1:**

- **Monday (Day 1):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Finger Independence** - Place your hand in a relaxed position on the fretboard, with fingers curled gently over the frets. Lift each finger one at a time, keeping the others still, to develop independent movement.
    - Exercise 2: **Chromatic Scales** - Play chromatic scales (all 12 half-steps within an octave) using both hands in unison, starting from any note. Focus on playing smoothly and evenly.
- **Tuesday (Day 2):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Finger Picking** - Hold a pick or use your fingers to play a simple pattern on the low E string. Focus on playing with a relaxed grip and smooth, even motion.
    - Exercise 2: **Arpeggios** - Break down chords into arpeggiated patterns (e.g., A minor: A-C-E). Play each note individually, focusing on smooth transitions between notes.
- **Wednesday (Day 3):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Finger Stretching** - Place your hand in a relaxed position on the fretboard. Slowly stretch each finger away from the others, keeping them still.
    - Exercise 2: **Alternate Picking** - Hold a pick or use your fingers to play a simple pattern on the low E string, alternating between two notes (e.g., A-D).
- **Thursday (Day 4):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Chromatic Scales in Reverse** - Play chromatic scales starting from a random note and moving up, then back down.
    - Exercise 2: **Barre Chords** - Practice playing basic barre chords (e.g., A, D) with a relaxed hand position.

**Week 2:**

- **Monday (Day 5):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Finger Independence in 3rds** - Place your hand in a relaxed position on the fretboard. Lift each finger one at a time, focusing on playing notes that are 3 frets apart.
    - Exercise 2: **Sweep Picking** - Hold a pick or use your fingers to play a simple pattern on multiple strings, sweeping from low E to high E string.
- **Tuesday (Day 6):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Arpeggios in Chords** - Break down chords into arpeggiated patterns. Focus on playing smoothly between notes, even when switching between chord shapes.
    - Exercise 2: **Fingerstyle Patterns** - Practice simple fingerstyle patterns using your thumb and index finger (e.g., a basic rock pattern).
- **Wednesday (Day 7):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Chromatic Scales in 4ths** - Play chromatic scales starting from a random note and moving up, then back down, using intervals of a 4th.
    - Exercise 2: **Bend and Release** - Practice playing single notes while bending them (using the whammy bar or vibrato) and then releasing them smoothly.
- **Thursday (Day 8):**
    - Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of finger stretches and chromatic scales
    - Exercise 1: **Finger Picking with Thumb** - Hold a pick or use your thumb to play a simple pattern on the low E string, while keeping the other fingers still.
    - Exercise 2: **Chromatic Scales in 5ths** - Play chromatic scales starting from a random note and moving up, then back down, using intervals of a 5th.
